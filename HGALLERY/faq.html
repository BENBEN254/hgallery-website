Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What products does HGALLERY offer?
HGALLERY offers a comprehensive range including glass, aluminium systems, shower cubicles, wall panels, picture frames, home decor, DIY products, power tools, hand tools, and camping gear. We supply premium quality materials for both residential and commercial projects.
Do you offer installation services?
Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our products. Our skilled team ensures precision and quality in every installation, from glass doors to aluminium windows and shower cubicles.
What are your business hours?
We are open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We are available on Sundays by appointment only. You can reach us via phone or email outside business hours.
Do you deliver across Kenya?
Yes, we deliver our products across Kenya. Delivery times and costs vary depending on location and order size. Contact us for a specific delivery quote for your area.
Can I get a custom quote?
Absolutely! We provide custom quotes for all projects. Simply contact us with your requirements, and our team will prepare a detailed quote tailored to your needs.
Do you offer warranties on your products?
Yes, all our products come with manufacturer warranties. Installation services are also backed by our quality guarantee. Specific warranty terms vary by product and will be discussed during consultation.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash payments. For larger orders, we can arrange installment payment plans with prior approval.
Do you offer custom fabrication?
Yes, we offer custom fabrication for glass and aluminium products. Our team can create bespoke solutions based on your specific requirements and measurements.
