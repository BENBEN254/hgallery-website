Our Story

Kenya's Trusted Glass & Aluminium Experts

Founded in 2015, HGALLERY LTD has grown from a small glass workshop into one of Kenya's most trusted suppliers of premium building materials and interior finishing solutions. Our journey is built on a simple philosophy: quality products, professional service, and customer satisfaction.

We specialize in supplying and installing premium glass, aluminium systems, shower cubicles, mirrors, wall panels, picture framing, home décor, DIY products, and power tools. Our experienced team provides complete solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial projects across Kenya.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality craftsmanship, integrity, and innovation. We partner with leading manufacturers to bring you the finest materials, and our skilled installation team ensures every project is completed to the highest standards.

Our Journey

The HGALLERY Story

A decade of growth, innovation, and excellence in the building materials industry.

2015

Foundation

HGALLERY LTD was established as a small glass workshop in Nairobi, serving local homeowners and contractors with quality glass products.

2017

Expanded Product Range

Added aluminium systems, shower cubicles, and mirrors to our product catalogue, becoming a comprehensive building materials supplier.

2019

New Showroom

Relocated to our flagship showroom at Our Mall, Magadi Road, with an expanded product display and demonstration area.

2021

Full-Service Provider

Launched professional installation services and expanded into home décor, DIY products, power tools, and camping gear, becoming a true one-stop shop.

2024

Digital Transformation

Launched our online presence to serve customers across Kenya, making it easier than ever to explore our products and request quotes and installations.

2026

Looking Ahead

Continuing to innovate and expand our offerings, with a focus on sustainability, quality, and customer satisfaction across East Africa.
